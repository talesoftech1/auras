Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Auras ("we", "us", or "our") provides AI-powered WhatsApp assistant services for small businesses in South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following information when businesses and their customers interact with Auras:

  • Business name, website URL, phone number, and email address (collected from public sources such as Google Maps)
  • WhatsApp phone numbers of individuals who message our AI assistants
  • Message content sent to and received from our AI assistants
  • Timestamps and interaction metadata

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve our AI WhatsApp assistant service
  • Build and train AI assistants on behalf of businesses
  • Send demo messages to businesses about our service
  • Respond to customer inquiries on behalf of businesses
  • Monitor service performance and prevent abuse

3. Data storage

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Conversation history is retained to provide context for ongoing AI conversations. Businesses can request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

4. WhatsApp messaging

Our service operates via the WhatsApp Business Platform. By interacting with an Auras-powered WhatsApp assistant, you consent to your messages being processed by our AI systems. We do not sell or share your WhatsApp conversations with third parties outside of the service providers necessary to operate Auras.

5. Third-party services

Auras uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic Claude — AI language model for generating responses
  • Meta WhatsApp Business API — messaging infrastructure
  • Supabase — database and data storage
  • Firecrawl — website content extraction

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

6. Data retention

We retain conversation data for as long as necessary to provide our service. Businesses that have not interacted with the platform for an extended period may have their data removed. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of receiving messages from Auras

8. Opting out

If you no longer wish to receive messages from Auras, you can reply "STOP" to any message or contact us directly. We will remove your number from our system within 48 hours.

9. Children's privacy

Our service is intended for use by businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify affected parties of significant changes. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Governing law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at hello@auras.co.za or visit auras.co.za.
